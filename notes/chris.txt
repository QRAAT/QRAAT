Access to serial port for selected users:
cat /etc/groups | grep /dev/ttyS0 # make sure there's no group already
groupadd serial 
chgrp serial /dev/ttyS0 /dev/ttyUSB0
chmod 0060 /dev/ttyS0
ls -l /dev/ttyS0

usermod -a -G serial <user> 

For all: 
chmod a+rw /dev/ttyS0

Ramdisk setup: 
Add the following line to /etc/fstab:
tmpfs   /tmp/ramdisk  tmpfs  nodev/nosuid,mode=1777,size=512M   0   0
Where the entries are separated by tabs \t.
