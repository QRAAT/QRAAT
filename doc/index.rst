Quail Ridge Automated Animal Tracking
=====================================

QRAAT is a radio frequency-based animal tracking system funded by the 
National Science Foundation and under development at the University of 
California at Davis. Our software is freely available under the terms of
the GNU General Public License and is published on 
`github <https://github.com/QRAAT/QRAAT>`_. 

This page provides software documentation of the system. To learn more 
about QRAAT and to view current site telemetry, visit 
`our project page <http://169.237.92.155>`_.
